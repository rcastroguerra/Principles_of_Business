1. **Question:**

**Matching Question:**  

Match the type of business ownership with how it is taxed.

| Business Ownership | Taxation |
|---|---|
| **Sole proprietorship** | **Individual income is taxed** |
| **Partnership** | **Individual owners' income is taxed** |
| **Corporation** | **Corporation and shareholders are taxed** |

**Explanation:**

- **Sole proprietorship:** The business is not taxed separately. The owner's profits are taxed as **personal income**.
- **Partnership:** Profits pass through to the **partners**, and each partner pays tax on their share of the income.
- **Corporation:** The **corporation pays corporate tax**, and shareholders also pay taxes on dividends, which is known as **double taxation**.

- The owners have unlimited liability
