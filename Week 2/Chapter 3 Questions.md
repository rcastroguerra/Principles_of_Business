**Question:**  

The advantage that exists when a country has a monopoly on producing a specific product is called:  

- arbitrage  
- comparative advantage  
- outsourcing  
- absolute advantage  

**Answer:**  
**absolute advantage**  

**Explanation:**  

An **absolute advantage** occurs when a country can produce a good more efficiently than any other country or is the only producer of that good. In this case, the country has a monopoly on production, giving it complete control over the supply and potential trade benefits.  

In contrast, **comparative advantage** refers to producing a good at a lower opportunity cost, not necessarily being the sole producer.

13. **Question:**

**Question:**  

A nation's balance of trade refers to  

- the total dollar value of exports  
- the total dollar value of imports  
- levels of trade in various production sectors  
- the difference in value between imports and exports  

**Answer:**  
**the difference in value between imports and exports** ✅  

**Explanation:**  

The **balance of trade** measures the difference between the value of a country's exports and imports over a specific period.  

- If exports exceed imports, the country has a **trade surplus**.  
- If imports exceed exports, the country has a **trade deficit**.  

It is not just the total of exports or imports individually, but the **net difference** between the two.
